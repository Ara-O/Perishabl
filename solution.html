Perishabl!

We have developed an app called Perishabl. With Perishabl, you'll be able to type in your groceries, and you'll get alerts whenever your grocery items are about to expire. You can also get tips/ideas on how to extend the lifespan of your grocery items

Features!


Add Grocery


With Perishabl, you'll be able to insert your grocery items into the app. It can help serve as a grocery list, but Perishabl also provides cheaper alternatives to the foods you buy, and stores a list of your groceries so you never forget what you want to buy
